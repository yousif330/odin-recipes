Bake a Cake For Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups butter
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons lemon extract
  • ¾ cup lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions

Step 1

Cream together the butter and sugar for 20 minutes. Add eggs, one at a time. Add flour, lemon extract and fold in the 7UP soft drin

Step 2

Pour into a well-greased 12 cup Bundt pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 60 to 75 minutes.

Step 3

Enjoy your meal :)
